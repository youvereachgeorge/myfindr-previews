Key Insights — Brisbane, March 2026
What's happening across Brisbane this month

🔥 March is running hot — activity jumped from February's pace

Brisbane planning applications have surged this month — significantly above what we saw in November and December. More projects are starting right now than at any other point recently. The window to get in front of new clients early is wide open.

📍 Camp Hill, Hendra, and Ascot are the hottest suburbs

These suburbs have each had multiple new applications land in the last 30 days — and Camp Hill's activity tripled week on week. They form a connected renovation and development corridor in inner Brisbane. If you work anywhere in this cluster, now is the time to be visible.

🔗 We now track projects from first application through to construction

When a property lodges a subdivision, then operational works, then a building permit — we connect all of those applications together. You can see exactly where each project sits in its lifecycle and when it becomes the right opportunity for your trade.
