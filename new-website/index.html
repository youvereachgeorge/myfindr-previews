1. How quickly can I launch with myFindr?

You can set up and go live in less than a day with our 1-click setup — no complex steps required. Focus on your content, and myFindr takes care of the structure so you can launch faster.

2. Do I need any design or coding experience?

Not at all. myFindr’s drag-and-drop editor makes everything visual and easy. Even beginners can adjust layouts without writing a single line of code.

3. Can I use myFindr for multiple projects?

Yes. One license lets you create unlimited personal or client projects. Perfect for freelancers and agencies managing multiple websites.

4. Does myFindr work on all devices?

Absolutely. myFindr is fully responsive, so your site will look and perform seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

5. What’s included with my purchase?

You’ll get the full template — all sections, pages, and features ready to use. Free lifetime updates ensure your site stays fresh and up to date.
