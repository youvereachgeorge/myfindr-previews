🔥 March 2026 is Brisbane's biggest month on record in this dataset

287 DAs lodged in a single month across Brisbane City Council — representing a wide spectrum from small renos through to major multi-dwelling and commercial developments. The pipeline is full and activity is accelerating heading into Q2.

These five suburbs each recorded 5 DAs this month — the highest per-suburb count in Brisbane. They span inner-south, inner-north, and eastern growth corridors. If your trade works anywhere near these areas, now is the time to build visibility.
