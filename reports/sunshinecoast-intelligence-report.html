📈 Sunshine Coast is tracking 26/month — strong and consistent in 2026

January through March 2026 has seen consistent DA lodgement across the Sunshine Coast. March alone recorded 78 applications — covering everything from residential dual occupancies through to major master-planned community updates. The coast's growth momentum continues strongly.

🏖️ Maroochydore continues to dominate as the region's commercial hub

With 12 DAs in 2026 (17 total in the dataset), Maroochydore remains the single most active suburb on the coast. It's followed closely by Buderim (10) and Beerwah (7) — reflecting both urban and growth-corridor activity across the region.

🏗️ Operational Works (75) leads all categories — infrastructure is moving

The dominance of Operational Works applications signals that approved developments are now moving into construction phase. This is the most valuable signal for trade suppliers, engineers, and construction firms — these projects are past the DA stage and actively building.

⚡ Operational Works (75) = construction phase has commenced

This is the most important signal for trade and construction businesses. Operational Works applications mean the DA has been approved and the developer is now lodging civil, drainage, and infrastructure plans. These projects are weeks away from breaking ground — this is your window to get in front of them.

🏘️ 55 Material Change of Use = next wave of approvals incoming

55 MCU applications in 2026 are currently working through the assessment pipeline. Many of these will become Operational Works applications in Q2-Q3 — meaning the Sunshine Coast's active construction pipeline is set to grow further through mid-2026. The backlog of opportunity is significant.
