Key Insights — March 2026
What's happening across both regions this month

🔥 This is the most active month for new projects we've seen

Planning applications have surged across Brisbane and the Sunshine Coast this month — significantly above what we saw in the previous two months. More projects are starting right now than at any other point in recent history. The window to get in front of new clients early is wide open.

📍 Camp Hill, Hendra, and Ascot are Brisbane's hottest suburbs

These three suburbs have each had multiple new planning applications land in the last 30 days — and Camp Hill's activity tripled week on week. They form a connected renovation corridor in inner Brisbane. If you work anywhere in this cluster, now is the time to be visible and proactive.

🌊 Maroochydore has been consistently busy for 6 months straight

The Sunshine Coast isn't spiking — it's consistently producing. Maroochydore in particular has been in the top tier of activity for half a year, with a strong lean toward commercial projects. A market that rewards long-term relationships over one-off deals.

🔗 We now track projects from first application right through to construction

When a property lodges a subdivision, then operational works, then a building permit — we connect all of those applications together. That means we can show you exactly where each project is in its lifecycle and when it becomes the right opportunity for you to get involved.
