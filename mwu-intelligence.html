🇦🇺 Australian Market Opportunities (for GM)
1
Corporate / Employer Wellness B2B2C
Sell MWU subscriptions as employee wellness benefits to AU companies. 12-month contracts = near-zero churn. No new product needed. ASX-listed corps increasingly mandated on mental + physical wellness benefits.
2
Annual Plan Conversion Push
If 30% of monthly subs convert to annual: ARR jumps ~15–20% with zero new customers. Annual = $199.99 vs $299.88 monthly equivalent. Push as a "founding member" campaign.
3
CSB AU Customer Cross-Sell
CSB AU already has an established customer base. An automated post-purchase sequence (CSB buyer → 7-day MWU free trial) could be the lowest-cost acquisition channel available. Same woman, zero extra ad spend.
4
Retreat & Events Programme Expansion
MWU ran Bali retreat in June 2024 (2nd edition). A structured AU events calendar (Gold Coast, Sydney, Melbourne) converts brand followers to loyal paying subscribers and creates organic content.
5
Private Health Insurance Integration
AU private health funds increasingly support wellness apps. Partnering with Medibank/Bupa to offer MWU as a subsidised benefit unlocks a new acquisition channel and adds institutional credibility.
6
Retention Engine — Loyalty & Milestones
Month 3–6 is the highest churn window in fitness apps. A milestone programme (90-day reward, CSB discount at 12 months) tied to both MWU + CSB would extend LTV and reduce churn meaningfully.
🇺🇸 USA Market Opportunities
1
CSB US Customer Database → MWU Trial
CSB ships worldwide including US. US CSB buyers are the highest-intent MWU prospects available — same brand trust, same demographic. Automated post-purchase email → 7-day MWU trial is the fastest US subscriber lever with zero paid acquisition.
2
US-Native Creator Programme
Rachel's audience is AU-dominant. Building a US coach roster (credible US fitness influencers as MWU coaches) would replicate the organic flywheel in the US market. TikTok is #1 fitness discovery in the US — largely untapped by MWU.
3
US Subscription Pricing Uplift
MWU's AU pricing ($24.99 AUD ≈ $15.50 USD) is likely underpriced for US market. SWEAT charged $19.99 USD/mo. A US-specific pricing tier could lift US ARPU by ~30% with no product changes.
4
28-Day Challenge Entry Product (US)
Rachel already runs "Get Event Ready in 28 Days." A US-targeted 28-day paid challenge ($29–49 one-off) is a low-friction entry product. Challenge completion → convert to monthly sub. High-converting funnel with urgency built in.
