It’s easier than ever to get ready for iPhone pre-order. Just pick the model you want and decide if you’d like to pay in full or monthly. Get the prep work done today so you can speed through checkout on 9.9.

Here’s how it works:

Open the Apple Store app, or visit apple.com.
