FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before booking a call.

What councils does myFindr cover?
myFindr currently covers Brisbane City Council and Sunshine Coast Council — two of the most active property markets in Queensland. We scrape both portals daily at 5am for new DA lodgements and approval updates.
How current is the data?
Data is refreshed every morning at 5am from council websites. By the time you log in at 8am, you're looking at intelligence that's less than 3 hours old — typically a full day ahead of competitors checking manually.
Which dashboard do I get access to?
Your dashboard is tailored to your trade. Interior Designers get the renovation DA dashboard. Builders and Developers get new build + corridor intelligence. Real Estate Agents get suburb hotspot and commercial DA tracking. You can discuss adding multiple views on your onboarding call.
What's in the weekly DA report?
Every Monday, you receive a curated report covering: new DA lodgements in your target suburbs, approvals and rejections, active developer movements, suburb activity rankings, and any notable changes from the week prior.
How does onboarding work?
After booking a call, we'll walk you through the platform, configure your suburb filters, and set up your weekly report preferences. Onboarding takes about 30 minutes and is included with your subscription at no extra cost.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There are no lock-in contracts. You can cancel at any time and your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
